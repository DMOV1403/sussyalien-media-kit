About the Channel

NOT YOUR AVERAGE
ANIMATION CHANNEL

"Where internet memes meet Arab street wisdom."

Sussy Alien blends Wojak-style meme animation with sharp social commentary — creating stories that feel pulled straight from Egyptian and Arab daily life. The art is deliberately raw. The humor is dark and honest. Viewers keep coming back because they’ve never seen their reality reflected like this.

The channel runs a recurring cast of recognizable archetypes — the struggling Egyptian expat, the numb office worker, the disillusioned football fan — all drawn in a hand-crafted style mixing Wojak and NPC internet culture with original Arabic storytelling.

Series like يوميات مصري مغترب (Diary of an Egyptian Expat), معاناة (The Suffering Series), and standalone satirical shorts regularly hit 50K–100K+ views.
